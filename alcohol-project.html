Alcohol Capstone Project¶

The overall purpose of this project is to gather data on the amount of alcohol people over the age of 15 consumed in each country in the world, compare correlations between them, and provide statistical analysis on whether or not temperature has an effect on the amount of alcohol consumed.

Analytical Questions:
1.Which type of alcohol is consumed the most in the world?
2.Which continent in the world consumes the most alcohol?
3.Is there a correlation between average country temperature and total alcohol consumed?

Hypothesis:
I believe that spirits will be the type of alcohol that is consumed the most in the world due to its wide selection. I believe that Europe will be the continent that consumes the most alcohol in world and that colder countries will consume more alcohol than warmer countries.

# This parses through the data sets and puts a country's index with the corresponding continent. It also creates a new
# data frame combining country with continent, beer, wine, and sprit averages. 

dic = {}
dic_temp = {}
country = []
continent = []
beer = []
wine = []
spirit = []
total_alcohol = []
temp = []
for i in range(len(df["country"])):
    for j in range(len(df2["name"])):
        if df["country"][i] == df2["name"][j]:
            dic[i] = df2["region"][j]
dic[184] = "Americas"
dic[182] = "Europe"

for key in dic:
    country.append(df.loc[key, "country"])
    continent.append(dic[key])
    beer.append(df.loc[key, "beer_servings"])
    wine.append(df.loc[key, "wine_servings"])
    spirit.append(df.loc[key, "spirit_servings"])
    total_alcohol.append(df.loc[key, "total_litres_of_pure_alcohol"])

df3 = pd.DataFrame()
df3["country"] = country
df3["continent"] = continent
df3["beer"] = beer
df3["wine"] = wine
df3["spirit"] = spirit
df3["total_liters"] = total_alcohol

for i in range(len(df3["country"])):
    for j in range(len(df_temp["Country Name"])):
        if df3["country"][i] == df_temp["Country Name"][j]:
            temp.append(df_temp["Average yearly temperature"][j])
            dic_temp[df3["country"][i]] = df_temp["Average yearly temperature"][j]
    if df3["country"][i] not in dic_temp:
        dic_temp[df3["country"][i]] = None
        temp.append(None)

df3["avg_temp"] = temp
